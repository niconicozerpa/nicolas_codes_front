Infobae.com

Almost 2 milion people visit Infobae.com very day. This heavily visited website is one of the most important news sites in Argentina and Latin America.

When I worked at the VI-DA Agency from Buenos Aires, I was responsible, together with my coworker Julia, for redesigning the site and building an integral system to manage its contents.

This system not only had to deal with millions of daily visits, but it also must allow the journalists to update the articles and media as quickly as possible.

Although it was the most stressful job I ever had, it has also been the most rewarding project as well.

Halsbrook.com

When e-commerce is more than e-commerce. Created in 2012, Halsbrook is an online shop that offers luxury clothing and accessories from top American an European designers. I've worked in this project through the VN Studios agency from Buenos Aires, Argentina.

But the underlying system behind Halsbrook does much more than just selling clothes. It manages the entire lifecycle of the products, from the initial purchase from the vendors until it reaches the final custoner.

Working like an ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning), the Halsbrook site also manages the inventory, file taxes, handle product shipping, and generates multiple reports for accounting, inventory and planning purposes.

InvGate

InvGate is a centralized service to manage all the IT assets (i.e. desktop computers, notebooks, or mobile devices) of a company network. It is possible to install software across all devices, monitor changes, and analyze which programs are used and how often.

When I worked in this company, I was responsible for mantaining and improving the software, which is used by Nike Argentina, Starbucks Argentina and other companies.
